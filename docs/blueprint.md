# **App Name**: Du Carmo Delícias Mineiras

## Core Features:

- Hero Section Display: Displays the main headline, subheadline, hero image, and primary 'Fale com a gente' WhatsApp Call to Action to immediately engage visitors.
- About Us & Experience Section: Presents a compelling narrative about Armazém e Empório Du Carmo's tradition, ambiance, and location, evoking a sense of local pride and comfort.
- Delicacies Showcase: Showcases key product categories like 'Cafeteria', 'Delícias Quentinhas', 'Doces e Sobremesas', and 'Cestas e Empório' using visually appealing blocks without revealing prices, driving desire for an in-store visit or WhatsApp inquiry.
- WhatsApp Call to Action Integration: Prominently places multiple WhatsApp buttons ('Deseja encomendar? Chame no WhatsApp', 'Fale com a gente') throughout the page, directly connecting users for inquiries, basket customization, or delivery orders.
- Localisation & Map Integration: Clearly presents the store's address and operating hours. Includes an embedded Google Maps display with a direct 'Como chegar' link to facilitate physical visits.
- Social Media Links: Provides easy access to the Empório's Instagram profile for further engagement and content discovery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, sophisticated black (#000000) to convey timeless elegance and strong brand presence.
- Background color: Crisp, clean white (#FFFFFF) for maximum readability and a premium, minimalist feel.
- Accent color: A subtle yet strong dark grey (#333333) for highlighting important elements and calls to action, maintaining monochrome harmony.
- Headlines font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for an elegant, traditional, yet contemporary feel that speaks to the empório's heritage.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (grotesque sans-serif) for clean readability and a modern, professional contrast with the headlines.
- Utilize subtle, line-art or minimalist icons that reflect the handcrafted nature of products and a cozy, traditional ambiance.
- A responsive layout optimized for all devices, featuring clear content hierarchy, ample whitespace, and prominent, accessible call-to-action buttons for high conversion.
- Employ subtle fade-in and slide-up animations for sections as the user scrolls, creating a polished and engaging browsing experience.
